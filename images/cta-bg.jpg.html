About Company

WHERE DREAMS TURN TO OPPORTUNITY

Homedestino has adequately grown in itself ever since its foundation day. The IRM concentrated mainly on the Retail sector, but today it has diversified itself by entering many other segments too. It has matured from time to time handling massive infrastructure projects, not only in Punjab but also in Himachal Pradesh.

The first real success was achieved by Homedestino with the completion of The Gulmohar Grand Hotel Project, in which Homedestino has achieved 100% bonus. After that, Homedestino developed a new team for brand leasing and commercial concept design. Homedestino is the only company that started street mall shopping complex with a food court and gaming zone under one roof which gave a kick start and boost to the company, and now Homedestino is coming with many new B2C and B2B projects in Punjab.

History

The history of any substance won't be worth remembering if it hasn't been attained successfully, and history demands perseverance and perspiring efforts for the same. Our history has been a journey of accumulating goodwill for the company through deliberate attempts for execution and reliability.

The initiation of "Shri Anand Hardware" took place in 1980 as a retail venture by the late Shri Rakesh Kumar Khanna. In 1992, we added other building material segments in retail and in 2000 elder son Mr. Rajesh Khanna had joined his father and expanded the retail business with many top-end international building material brands in different categories.

His younger son, Mr. Rajeev Khanna, had also joined both in 2005 with innovative business mind and started an Integrated controlled-atmosphere Mushroom growing unit in Nawanshahr and also start getting turn-key interior projects. The venture stands firm with the support of concrete pillars attached to it in the form of meticulously veteran professionals; who are they for their action and has the will to survive. Collective assembling of such traits and revelation has brought "Homedestino" into the picture. All three associate partners are competent enough to let their empire run with optimum results.

Our Vision

We have been in the business of building materials for the last 35 years, and during this period we have dealt with our clients who have undergone a drastic change in their choices and expectations and awareness about the latest brands and ornamental materials being used in the modern construction of high-quality houses.
